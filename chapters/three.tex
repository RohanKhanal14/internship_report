\subsection{Roles and Responsibilities}

During my internship at \textbf{Kendrabindu Online Pvt. Ltd.}, I performed the following roles and responsibilities in the News Portal Administration and Technical Content Management Department:

\begin{itemize}

    \item \textbf{CMS Management:} Managed and maintained the Content Management System (CMS), including user roles, access permissions, and editorial workflows.

\item \textbf{Content Publishing and Scheduling:} Assisted in publishing, scheduling, and updating news articles, ensuring timely and error-free content delivery.

\item \textbf{Website Performance Monitoring:} Monitored server uptime, page load speed, and database health, applying optimizations when necessary.

\item \textbf{Security Management:} Implemented password policies, input validation, and basic protection measures against cyber threats.

\item \textbf{Troubleshooting and Maintenance:} Identified and resolved formatting errors, broken links, and system bugs.

\item \textbf{Multimedia Content Management:} Managed images, videos, and embedded media, ensuring optimization and responsive display.

\item \textbf{Web Analytics Monitoring:} Tracked user engagement, traffic patterns, and popular content to support content strategy.

\item \textbf{Social Media Integration:} Assisted in automating content sharing and monitoring audience engagement across social platforms.

\item \textbf{Technical Documentation:} Maintained documentation of CMS operations, database structure, and deployment workflows.

\end{itemize}

\subsection{Description of the Project Involved During Internship}

The Kendrabindu Online Portal (kendrabindu.com) is a web-based news and media platform designed to deliver timely news, multimedia content, and technical updates to a broad audience across Nepal and beyond. The portal provides a centralized content management system (CMS) for editors, reporters, and administrators to manage articles, images, videos, categories, and user engagement efficiently. The system supports role-based access control, scheduled content publishing, SEO optimization, and integration with social media platforms for wider content distribution.

During my internship, I worked on multiple technical tasks related to news portal administration, system optimization, and content management. Key contributions included maintaining the CMS structure, implementing content workflows, optimizing website performance and mobile responsiveness, monitoring server and database health, and securing the portal against common web vulnerabilities. I also assisted with analytics tracking, automated post scheduling, and documentation of system operations. These activities collectively aimed to enhance the reliability, usability, and operational efficiency of the Kendrabindu news portal.
